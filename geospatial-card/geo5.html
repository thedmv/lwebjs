Urban Green Space Analysis: New York City

Project Overview

This project is about exploring the viability and locations with the best potential for placing green spaces that enhance the overall makeup of the urban envelope. We use tubes going into space to help the heat balance with the core of there earth and the vegetation that is in the middle. The goal is to use Full-Column Effervesence to enable the feedback loop between organic and non-organic components.
